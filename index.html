The 6 Core Manifesto Pillars

Citizen-Centric Governance ka complete framework

Pillar 01

Preamble Sthiti Majbooti

"We, the People..." ke asli vaade ko zameen par utarna. Dharam, jaati, gender ya region ke bhedbhav ko poori tarah khatam karke sirf ek identity rakhna — INDIAN.

Pillar 02

Restructuring of Laws

Political parties ka kaam sirf vichardhara aur suggestions dena hoga, kanoon par kabza karna nahi. Active Presidential aur Preinformative Judicial system lagoo hoga.

Pillar 03

Direct Citizen Participation

Digital Referendum aur Feedback Mechanism se bade kanoonon par janta ki direct rai li jayegi. Har nagrik ke paas ek legal Citizen Protection Shield hogi.

Pillar 04

Subsidiary Active System

Annual Right to Recall: Netaon ke bhashan aur vaade Digital ID par record honge. Agar 1 saal ke audit mein kaam kharab raha, toh seat chhodni hogi aur party naya candidate degi.

Pillar 05

Accountable Judiciary

CJI ka chunaav President, Ruling party, aur Top 3 Opposition panel karega. Judges ke past records open rahenge, 1 saal ka performance audit hoga, aur post-retirement politics par ban hoga.

Pillar 06

Protected Administration

IAS/IPS officers well-executor banenge. Unhe political badli/dabao se bachaya jayega. Retired judges ki supreme team unka financial aur public-interaction audit karegi via Proactive RTI.
